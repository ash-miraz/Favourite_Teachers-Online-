Fahad Hossain

Fahad Hossain is a science teacher, founder of "Amader School" and an educator at North South University. He started his venture through social media in 2017 after completing MPH degree. He teaches physics, chemistry, and biology for SSC and HSC. He is my most favourite teacher and has a dedicated fan base among young students in Bangladesh.

Sajan Chakraborty

Sajan Sir is a highly skilled math teacher who specializes in teaching Math for SSC and HSC students. He is well-known for his ability to make complex math concepts easy to understand and his teaching style has helped many students succeed. He works on the popular Bangladeshi educational platform 10 Minute School.

Munzereen Shahid

Munzereen Shahid is a Bangladeshi author, speaker, English teacher and social media personality, who became popular after publishing English teaching videos on 10 Minute School, an online education platform. She is a well-known figure in the field of teaching English and seems to be well-liked by her students.
